Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Disclaimer: This document is for informational purposes. Consult a qualified attorney for legal advice specific to your situation.

1. Introduction

ClawBDR, LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and business data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

This policy applies to all users of the ClawBDR, LLC service, including visitors to our website and subscribers to our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Company name, title, and team size
  • Billing information (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers)

CRM & Email Data

  • CRM data accessed via read-only OAuth connections (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce) — contacts, companies, deals, and activities
  • Email metadata — subject lines, send/receive timestamps, and recipient information used to coordinate outreach and avoid duplicates

Usage Data

  • Feature usage, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Browser type, device information, and IP address
  • Pages visited on our website

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Power your AI BDR Agent — prospecting, research, outreach, CRM management, and monitoring
  • Improve the Product: Analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Communicate with You: Send account notifications, billing receipts, product updates, and support responses
  • Security & Compliance: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following categories of third-party services to operate ClawBDR:

  • AI Processing — Anthropic & OpenAI: Your data is sent to AI providers for processing (research, content generation, analysis). We select providers committed to not training on customer data. Their respective privacy policies and terms apply.
  • Billing — Stripe: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card information. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • CRM Providers — HubSpot, Salesforce: We connect to your CRM via OAuth with the permissions you grant. We access only the data necessary to provide the Service.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Isolation: Customer data is logically isolated per account. Your data is never commingled with other customers'.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols (AES-256, TLS 1.2+).
  • Location: Data is stored in the United States.
  • Access Controls: Internal access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained for as long as your subscription is active and your account is in good standing.
  • After Cancellation: Upon cancellation, you may request a data export. Data exports are not a free service — a fee will be assessed based on the scope of the request. All customer data is permanently deleted within 30 days of cancellation. If no export request is made within 30 days of cancellation, data will be deleted with no obligation to retain or recover it.
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain certain data longer if required by law or to resolve disputes.

7. We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or business data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other commercial purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

Period. No exceptions.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Session management — keeping you logged in
  • Analytics — understanding how our website and product are used (aggregated, anonymized)

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

9. Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale: We do not sell your data, so this right is automatically satisfied

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@clawbdr.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

ClawBDR, LLC is a B2B service and is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from someone under 18, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically.
